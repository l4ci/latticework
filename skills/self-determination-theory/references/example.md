# Example: self-determination-theory

**You invoke it with:**
> "A senior engineer on my team has quietly checked out since the reorg. Use SDT to figure out why and how to bring them back."

**It needs from you:**
- SUBJECT (required): "Sam, a strong senior backend engineer, four years on the team, until recently one of the most driven people on it."
- DOMAIN (required): "Their day-to-day engineering work: design, delivery, and code review on the payments platform."
- SIGNS (required): "Since the reorg three months ago: does the minimum, stopped volunteering for hard problems, terse in review, skips optional design discussions, no quality drop yet but the spark is gone."
- CONTEXT (optional): "The reorg introduced a new approval gate: every non-trivial change now needs sign-off from a central architecture council before it ships. Sam used to make those calls. Same team, same pay, same peers, who still like and respect them."

**You get back:** a saved markdown report (`self-determination-theory-<subject>-<date>.md`) with a need profile table (satisfaction, frustration, and bearing for Autonomy, Competence, Relatedness), the three need reads with tells and evidence, the binding need with the mislabeling trap named, where the subject sits on the controlled-to-autonomous motivation continuum, targeted need-supportive interventions with owners, and a re-check.

**See a full worked example:** [self-determination-theory-checked-out-engineer-2026-06-19.md](self-determination-theory-checked-out-engineer-2026-06-19.md), the report this invocation produces.
