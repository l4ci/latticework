# Example: ooda-loop

**You invoke it with:**
> "Run an OODA loop on a competitor that just slashed their price"

**It needs from you:**
- SITUATION (required): a larger competitor cut its plan price by 40% this morning, and our sales team is already getting "match this or we churn" emails from key accounts
- CONTEXT (optional): we are a smaller analytics SaaS, our edge is onboarding and support, we can read churn signals and trial conversions daily, and the board meets in two weeks

**You get back:** a saved markdown loop log (`ooda-loop-competitor-price-cut-<date>.md`) with one block per loop (Observe, Orient with the model before, the mismatch, and the model after, then Decide, and Act and result), a tempo note on whether you are getting inside the competitor's loop or falling behind it, and caveats.

**See a full worked example:** [ooda-loop-competitor-price-cut-2026-06-19.md](ooda-loop-competitor-price-cut-2026-06-19.md) (the report this invocation produces).
